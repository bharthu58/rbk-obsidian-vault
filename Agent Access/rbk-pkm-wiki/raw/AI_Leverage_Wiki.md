# 🚀 AI Leverage Strategy: Reference Guide

## 🛠 Tool Matrix & Assignment

| Tool | Tier | Role | Best For... |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **ChatGPT** | **Free** | **The Intern** | Quick lookups, basic syntax, drafting prompts, and non-sensitive triage. |
| **Gemini** | **Plus** | **The Librarian** | Massive context (1M+ tokens), Google ecosystem integration, log analysis, and long doc research. |
| **Claude** | **Pro** | **The Architect** | High-level C++ reasoning, complex architecture, deep debugging, and agentic terminal coding. |

---

## 🔄 The "Low-Latency" Workflow
*Always escalate from cheapest to most powerful.*

1.  **[TRIAGE]** Start in **ChatGPT (Free)** to clarify requirements or get a "rough draft" of a solution.
2.  **[GROUND]** Use **Gemini (Paid)** to ingest large amounts of data (documentation, logs, codebase) and find relevant facts.
3.  **[IMPLEMENT]** Take the refined prompt and facts to **Claude (Pro)** for the final, production-ready code or strategy.

---

## 💡 Efficiency Rules (Token Management)
* **The "Drafting" Hack:** Do 80% of the conversational "back-and-forth" in ChatGPT Free. Only move to Claude/Gemini when the prompt is 100% ready.
* **Context Reset:** Start a **NEW CHAT** for unrelated tasks. Long thread histories consume your subscription "high-usage" tokens faster.
* **The Ecosystem Rule:** Use Gemini specifically for anything requiring Google Keep/Docs integration to keep your "Technical Wiki" updated automatically.
